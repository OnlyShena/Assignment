About Us

Discover our story when it comes to getting the delicious food and drinks you need.

Our Story

Founded in 2005, Safari Food Cabin began as a humble food truck parked on the busy streets of a vibrant city. With a passion for culinary exploration and a vision to bring diverse flavors to food lovers, our journey began. Over the years, our commitment to quality ingredients, innovative recipes and exceptional customer service has allowed us to grow from a single food truck. A food and beverage company with several branches across the country. At the heart of our success is a talented team of chefs, each with a unique background and passion for creating exceptional dishes. Our menu is a fusion of global cuisine, carefully crafted for every palate. From the amazing spices of the Indian subcontinent to the delicate pastas of Italy and the refreshing flavors of Southeast Asia to the sweet delights of desserts from around the world, every bite tells a story. As we continue our journey, we are committed to exploring new culinary horizons, embracing cultural diversity and creating an unforgettable dining experience for our beloved customers.

Our Mission

At Safari Food Cabin, our mission is to tantalize your taste buds with a world of flavors. We strive to:

  • Create exceptional dishes that reflect the authenticity and richness of various cuisines.
  • Source the finest ingredients to ensure the highest quality and freshness in every bite.
  • Provide a warm and welcoming dining experience that captures the spirit of exploration and adventure.
  • Share our passion for food and culture, fostering connections and memories around the table.

Our Vision

At Safari Food Cabin, our vision is to be the leading provider of extraordinary culinary experiences. We aim to redefine the way people enjoy food by blending innovation with tradition.We envision a world where every meal is an opportunity to embark on a gastronomic adventure. Our commitment to quality, authenticity, and creativity drives us to continuously push the boundaries of flavor and presentation.Whether you're dining in our cozy restaurant or indulging in our takeout offerings, our vision is to leave you with a sense of culinary delight that lingers long after the last bite.
